Results & Future Vision

Results: Achieving Predictive Excellence

01

High Model Accuracy:

Logistic Regression achieved an accuracy of 91.2%, while Random Forest and XGBoost models outperformed with 95.1% and 94.9%, respectively. The ensemble approach combining these models further enhanced overall performance, demonstrating exceptional consistency and reliability.

02

Insightful Feature Analysis:

Important factors such as applicant income, loan amount, and loan purpose were identified as critical predictors. Higher incomes and smaller loan amounts significantly increased approval likelihood. Our models revealed that loan applications for debt consolidation or home improvement had higher approval rates, highlighting industry-specific risk assessments.

03

Enhanced Risk Identification

The ensemble approach improved recall and F1 scores, crucial for accurately identifying high-risk applicants. This minimizes false positives and false negatives, providing a balanced and precise evaluation.
